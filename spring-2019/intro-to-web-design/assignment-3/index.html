Avatar Aang

About Contact Info

I am just a peaceful young monk who lived with his masters at the northern air temple. I have tattoos that symbolize my proficieny with the art of airbending but far greater things were written for my future. After learning of my destiny as the avatar, I ran away not knowing of the impending fire nation attack on my people. While running away, my bison and I were caught in a storm and ultimately frozen in ice for a century. After disappearing from the world for 100 years, it is my job as the avatar to learn the 4 elements, restore balance, and bring order to the world.
