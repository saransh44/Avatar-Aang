Avatar Aang

I am just a peaceful young monk who lived with his masters at the northern air temple. I have tatoos that symbolize my profecieny with the art of airbending but far greater things were written for my future. After learning of my destiny as the avatar, I ran away not knowing of the impending fire nation attack on my people. While running away, my bison and I weren caught in a storm and ultimately frozen in ice for a century. After disappearing from the world for a 100 years, it is my job as the avatar to learn the 4 elements, restore balance, and bring order to the world.
