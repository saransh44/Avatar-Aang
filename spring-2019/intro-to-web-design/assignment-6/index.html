An Unfortunate Turn of Events: Rise of Antisemitism in America

Unfortunatly, antisemitism has turned into a major issue in America. Antisemitism is a certain perception of Jews, which may be expressed as hatred toward Jews. Rhetorical and physical manifestations of antisemitism are directed toward Jewish or non-Jewish individuals and/or their property, toward Jewish community institutions and religious facilities. There are two notable attacks which you can read about from the above links. However, the biggest problem is that government leaders and the 3 branches are not using the full force of their power to squash the brewing hatred and deter other hate crimes being commited against those of the Jewish faith.
